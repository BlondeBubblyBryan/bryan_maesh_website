Frequently Asked Questions

Payouts here at Maesh are fast - you can receive your payouts within a few business days, or even the same day. And for your first payout, there is no week long delay to get started.

Maesh is currently focused on PayNow. If you do need to accept credit card payments, we recommend that you have another payment gateway alongside Maesh. Do put the Maesh payment gateway on the top of the list of payment methods - our merchants report better conversion rates when Maesh is on top 😉

Since we use bank apps and not credit cards, you won't have to worry about paying the expensive chargeback fees when you use Maesh.

We are always looking to support more merchants - if you don't see your specific platform here, do still reach out! We will let you know if support is coming your way.

Depending on your platform, existing plugins can work with Maesh - for example, the Xero plugin on WooCommerce works perfectly with Maesh.

For webstores like WooCommerce or Prestashop, Maesh automatically updates the order when payments are received. For bespoke webstores, we provide API endpoints for merchants to check payment status.

If you have more questions about Maesh, do reach out to us at hello@maesh.io . We would be happy to assist you!
